Our Activities

Ironing under the sea!

One of the clubs activities involves scuba diving with a twist! Join other members of the Bournemouth Xtreme Ironing club in an exciting underwater adventure whilst getting your pesky ironing done.

Mountain Ironing

Love mountain climbing? Love ironing? Personally I love neither. Come join the Bournemouth Xtreme Ironing crew as we trek up the Rivelin rocks to complete our most treasured chore.

High speed ironing

Adrenline junkies look out! This weekly excursion takes place in middle of the motorway and can be very dangerous so be prepared to be out of your comfort zone
